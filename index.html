OUR amenities

Experience the luxury and finest hospitality in Aryan Inn, Lalitpur, Uttar Pradesh where we provide you the best accommodation with a host of comforts and services. Located on the Jhansi Sagar Highway, the hotel is most suitable for business as well as family leisure.

Rooms and experience

World Class
Facilities

Stylishly modern in décor and finished with opulent detailing, Aryan Inn’s 30 guestrooms and 4 suites features spacious layouts, comfortable stays and large windows that frame stunning garden view. Each room has been carefully designed to offer a space of calm and peace. Choose this as your perfect getaway destination and you will never feel away from HOME.

Surreal Interiors

Celebrations

Aaryan Inn brings together modern event venues, innovative services and style offering two gardens, each having a gathering capacity for about 1,000 people and two event halls which can accommodate 300 people.

The private garden space can be adapted for meetings, enchanted celebrations, intimate weddings or lavish parties. From the moment you chose us for organising your wedding, we assist you with every detail, making your event memorable and romantic.Our Special Events team is available to assist with table settings, floral arrangements, décor and any other creative services you may need.

Dining Experience

The Aryan Inn restaurant is a convivial dining experience which offers a sophisticated atmosphere for guests to enjoy breakfast, lunch or dinner throughout the day.

Menus combining unique tastes and flavours driven by seasonal change focuses on imaginative, no waste, healthy lifestyle options. The different cuisines offered are Indian, Chinese, Italian and continental. Guests are invited to relax and enjoy some quality time to spark their tastebuds.
